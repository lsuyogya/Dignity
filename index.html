About Dignity
Initiative

Dignity Initiative is a Kathmandu-based not-for-profit, not-government organization, whichworks on knowledge production on the issues of social exclusion and human rights with a special focus one Dalit. The Dalit community shares 13.2 percent of the total national population of Nepal. They are politically excluded, economically deprived, and socio-culturally discriminated. They have been struggling for more than seventy years to claim Dalit as equal as other human beings.
